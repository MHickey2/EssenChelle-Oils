About Us

Welcome to EssenChelle Oils

EssenChelle Oil started as a small enterprise in 2019 and was initially a market stall at a bootmarket. With a growing interest, I decided to start an online presence. I created a small blog with links to where I could be contacted for the sale of a number of essential oils. As demand grew the range got bigger and I had a dedicated website created to deal with the increasing sales. The range has also grown to include massage oils and aromotherapy Oil burners.

The Blog focuses on the products and will help users find the oils that would benefit them most. I love to have contact with potential customers, and would love you to contact me if you require any further information on the products, or if you would like me to extend my product range further. Sign up for the monthly EssenChelle Oils Newsletter (form located in footer) for all the lastest information, and to learn about special offers exclusive to our subscribers. So enjoy the Site and I can't wait for you to try our Products, I am sure you will have a great Experience.
