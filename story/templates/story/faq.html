Frequently Asked Questions


Shopping with EssenChelle-Oils is easy, safe and secure. You can shop with confidence because all goods purchased carry an Unconditional 30 Day Money-back Guarantee!

If you cannot find an answer to your query below please contact us on our contact page for further help.

Ordering

We understand if you are a new custoer you may have questions about ordering products with EssenChelle Oils, so we have included some questions that you may need answers for, if you have anymore please contact us, we will be glad to help.

Do I need to open an account to place an order?

You don't need to already have an account before you can order, you simply can add your details at the checkout page.

How will I know my order has been accepted?

When your order is processed yo will be presented with the checkout success page with the full details of your order, your billing information and your delivery details. You will also have a pop up message letting you know your order was successfully processed. You will also receive an auto-generated order acknowledgement email within minutes of placing your order which will outline all the details of the order that you have placed. let us know immediately if you have made a mistake with your order so that we can amend it for you.

I'm having trouble finding a product

Use the 'Search' facility located in the header of all product pages, and type in the name of the product you are looknig for. Your results will appear in order of relevance, according to your search term.

Can I see my previous orders?

Yes. Log into Your Account where you will find a history of all your previous orders in your profile page listed by date.

How do I remove items in my basket?

To view the contents of your basket select the bag icon which is located at the top of every page. In the shopping bag page in the 'Qty' checker change the quantity to the desired number and click 'Update'. You will have an opportunity to adjust your bag throughout the purchasing process.

Which payment cards do you accept?

We accept the following credit and debit cards:

  • Mastercard
  • Visa
  • Visa Debit
  • Visa Electron
  • UK Maestro
  • PayPal

Dispatch and Delivery

When you place an order it will be processed in an efficient and professional manner. Orders are usually processed and dispatched within 24 hours of receipt, or up 48 hours during busy periods such as after a weekend or Public Holiday.

Below you will find most of the common questions regarding deliveries, so please be sure to read all of the sections below. If you are unable to find the answer to your query in the list below, contact us for further help.

How long do deliveries take?

After processing, orders are dispatched via one of our contracted Carrier Services or An Post. The speed of the delivery is determined by the distance you live from Ireland and the efficiency of your country's internal mail network.

Providing the address details you supplied with your order were complete and correct, deliveries should normally take;-

  • FREE Delivery (Standard Service) - 3 to 4 business days after dispatch.
  • Priority Service - 2 to 3 business days after dispatch.
  • Express Service - 24 hours after dispatch, available Monday to Friday only.
  • placed prior to 2.00pm on Fridays using the Express Service will be delivered on Monday, or the next working day in the case of Bank Holidays etc.

If you think you will not be at home to take delivery please provide an alternative delivery address where there will be somebody available during the day. To do this, simply fill out the details in the 'Delivery Address' section on the Checkout page.

A product has arrived damaged. What should I do?

Keep all the packaging materials. If your carton was severely damaged in transit you must inform the Carrier Company/Postal Service immediately. Next, contact us so that we can help you sort things out and get replacement products out to you.

Returns and Refunds

We have tried to cover all of the common questions within this subject, so before you email us please read the information below. If you cannot find the answer to your query in the list below, Contact Us for further help.

EssenChelle Oils wants you to be completely happy with your purchase, so if for any reason you are not 100% satisfied we are happy to offer a refund, exchange or replacement on any item returned to us within thirty days of your receipt of goods, provided they are still in their original packaging, undamaged and in an unused condition.

If you are returning an item because of an error on our part we will be happy to refund all delivery charges incurred. Otherwise you will be responsible for those charges and the costs of any other services provided to you in connection with your purchase.

Refunds

We will notify you via e-mail of your refund once we've safely received, checked and processed the returned item(s) according to our policy, above. If you are returning an item that was not due to an error on our part, the cost of shipping that item to you will be deducted from your refund.

Import Duties

Overseas customers may be subject to import duties or taxes which are levied once your order arrives in your country. These costs, and any additional charges for customs clearance must be borne fully by you since we have no control over these charges and cannot predict what they may be.
